Education begins in Kindergarten, it is easy to forget this.

And the kids that never give up never take a day off.

It begins with Mentors, Inspiration, Friendship.

We must face policy that does not make sense, that will never make sense; due to persistent malformed-understanding of education.

Misunderstanding of the gravity of Poverty.

And kids that stink - "It is hard to teach kids who stink."

There is no education without human connection, but there is merit in apps, computer programs, videos, audio books, and overhead internet satellites.

We all need an aim, both educators, and the kids.

There is no clearer aim than becoming a Business Creator, Founder, and escaping Poverty. Rising to no end in sight, Eventually leaving a legacy, unbreakable shoulders for giants to stand upon.

It is easy to forget that we have no limits.

It is easy to believe the lie that we can stop, because we know enough, or got enough; or had enough.

It is easy to misunderstand the purpose of Education.

We don't need a generation of Brilliant and Unstoppable Teachers, or Policy Makers.

Oh, there will never be enough of those.

We need and endless supply of Brilliant and Unstoppable Students who accept the responsibility for their own education, at their own pace, and a sequence that matches who they are.

Grades must end. Too many children confuse grades they get, with their own value to the world.

The only reason why someone would ever fail a test, is that the wisdom they already had was incompatible with what was being taught. To force incompatible knowledge on someone and blame them for not integrating it, is evil.

If you are young and still in school. Begin making unbreakable promises that you will fix what is broken. And if you have already left it all behind, now it is time to remember your hopes, to go back with your wisdom of today, and fix what was broken when you were little.

The cradle of education is within. The flame of curiosity, lives within. Students reach out and put things into their soul, all the time.

Sometimes all it takes is a poem.

Sometimes just a note that all education is for is beating back Poverty of Mind and Wallet that they were unfortunately and unfairly dropped into, and now they must learn wisdom and fight their way out, and then bring back the light so that others may follow, and finally enjoy their existence and high achievement, and leave a legacy worth a following.

It is never for good grades, or a good job; and that's the nonsense we teach them.

Education is for the opposite of Memorization and Servitude, it is for Creativity and Freedom.

And the opposite of high achievement is not failing while daring greatly.

Failure is nonsense, just another excuse not to try.

There is no such thing as failure, because every step forward can only add to success.
