Side Project Idea

Sell your 3D models through Printing on Demand (POD, printing only when someone pays for your product) at Shapeways and similar 3d print shops. Shapeways created shapejs a programmable product creator with many examples in JavaScript, this can be used to generate a large number of unique products (antique coin pendants, tiny statues of art in public domain, odd bobble heads, skeleton wallets, waveform rings, UPC/QR code bracelets or keychain doodads.). Take a look at their marketplace for more ideas.

You are not limited to digital models, you can experiment with 3d scanners as well.
